Community Guidelines

ShesWithUs is a space where every woman can feel safe, valued, and empowered. We are a supportive community where women provide mutual support for one another, seek or share perspectives, obtain valuable resources and skills, and develop meaningful lasting connections. We inclusively welcome all who identify as a woman and are dedicated to embracing our intersectional identities. Our values are grounded in mutual respect of one another regardless of race, religion, ethnicity, sexual orientation, age, etc.

By joining our community, you agree to:

  • Respect one another and appreciate each others' differences.
  • Empathize by actively listening and understanding each others' unique perspectives.
  • Share your thoughts, feelings, and opinions without shame or judgement
  • Positivity by using thoughtful and supportive language
  • Empower and elevate each other
  • Confidentiality by being mindful of protecting the anonymity of each others' shares
  • Trust that many truths can coexist
